Welcome to the School of life!

A school which is determinted to create a happy world. In times like this humans are losing their humanity and treating each other as utility, we yearn to rekindle the love and companssion amoung our intelligent race. We keep forgetting our values and small acts that keeps are going as a society.

We at Life 2.0 believe it's time to change how schooling works. We are not taught how to handle stressful situations or how to think and do the greater good. We wish to change the face of the world and create a better one for our kids.

We welcome you all to this wonderful school. Did I say all, yes indeed! There is no bar on our student's age cause learning should never stop.

So join us if you:

  • Want to rekindle the baby-like happiness within you.
  • Yearn of better financial planning.
  • Dream of creating a happy home.
  • Wish to be part of a co-operative society.
  • Desire to change this world into a wonderful place.
