Welcome to the School of life!

A school which is determinted to create a happy world. In times like this, when humans are losing their humanity and treating each other as utility, we yearn to rekindle the love and companssion amoung our intelligent race. We keep forgetting our values and small acts that keeps are going as a society. We at 'School of Life' believe it is time to change how schooling works. We are taught the complex world history and calculus, but our syllabus lacks the basics. We are not taught to handle stress or how to set our priorities. We wish to change the face of the world and create a better one for our kids.

The corporate world is shifting as well. Now even the organisations do not only look for the talent but also judges the candidates on how well they will acustom to their values. You have to be a team player and a motivator.

We welcome you all to this wonderful school. Did I say all, yes indeed! There is no bar on our student's age cause learning should never stop. Our classrooms are deisgned for all ages. We hope to see you at some point of our journey.

So join us if you :

  • Want to rekindle the baby-like happiness within you.
  • Yearn of better financial planning.
  • Dream of creating a happy home.
  • Wish to be part of a co-operative society.
  • Desire to change this world into a wonderful place.
