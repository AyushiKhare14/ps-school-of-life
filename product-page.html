Peace Out!

How to live happily with mother in law.

We all wish to live in harmony, but sometimes life and people around present us with situations, which are extremely difficult to handle. We try our best yet we fail. This classroom is about handling one such situation.The infamous mother-in-law and daughter-in-law feud.

What does this couse offers:
  • 10 days of interactive sessions for understanding the situation.
  • 20 days of 30 minute recorded classroom to address and mitigate the problem.
  • Daily excercises to give a develop a better perspective.
